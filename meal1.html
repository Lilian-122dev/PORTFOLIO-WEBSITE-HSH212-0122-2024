Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. While the pasta cooks, fry the pancetta or bacon until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and return it to the pot. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the hot pasta and mix well.
  5. Add the cooked pancetta and garlic to the pasta, toss everything together.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with parsley if desired.

Nutritional Information (Approximate per serving)

  • Calories: 500
  • Protein: 20g
  • Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Fat: 20g
