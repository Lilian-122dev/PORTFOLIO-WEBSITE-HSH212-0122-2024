Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt and oil as required

Instructions

  1. Soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then cook until 70% done.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Set some aside for garnishing.
  3. Add whole spices, ginger-garlic paste, and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, and biryani masala. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add chicken pieces, yogurt, and salt. Cook until the chicken is tender.
  6. In a deep pot, layer the partially cooked rice and chicken masala alternately. Sprinkle fried onions and herbs between layers.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat ("dum") for 20 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Nutritional Information (Approximate per serving)

  • Calories: 650
  • Protein: 35g
  • Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Fat: 25g
